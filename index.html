About Me

A professional aviator by trade, I am also fluent in data science. I'm proud to serve my country in the Navy Reserve and am thankful for the unwavering support of my civilian employer.

I'm always looking for efficiencies in existing systems. I enjoy spreading the love of data and its ability to improve processes to anyone willing to invest time into learning and growth.

I run Fleet Logistics Support Wing's Mission Marketplace: a software suite for managing a global fleet of logistics aircraft performing worldwide missions. I've designed and built essentially the entire system, from a Dataverse back end to numerous flows in Power Automate and Power Query, to the user interface in Power Apps.
